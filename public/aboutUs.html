Popular questions

How to order if our address is not in your area delivery?

If your address is outside your delivery zone, you can:

  1. Pick up the order by pickup at one of our addresses. (In this case you will receive "Baked mono maki with salmon as a gift).
  2. Find out from the operator whether it is possible to make an exception for delivery outside the zone c present moment time.
What time is it wait for the order?

Delivery time depends on the order address.
Delivery to the green zone - up to 1 hour.
Delivery to the yellow zone - up to 1.5 hours.
Delivery to the red zone - up to 2 hours.

Is shipping free?

Yes, delivery is free with a minimum order amount of $20 in the green delivery zone, $35 in the yellow zone and $50 in the red zone.

What time do you accept orders?

We accept orders every day from 11:00 a.m. to 10:30 p.m.
