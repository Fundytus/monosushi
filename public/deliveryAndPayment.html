Delivery and payment

Green area

We deliver within the green zone 1 hour

The minimum order amount in this zone is 20 $

Yellow area

We deliver within the yellow zone 1.5 hour

The minimum order amount in this zone is 35 $

Red area

We deliver within the red zone 2 hour

The minimum order amount in this zone is 50 $

The Monosushi team offers an extremely convenient delivery and payment system. The clarity of ours work standards ensures fast and high-quality fulfillment of orders for our guests.

We divided Winnipeg into conventional delivery zones - green, yellow and red. For each zone we are clear we determine the time delivery
The green delivery zone is closer to our kitchen, so orders to this zone are delivered within 1 hour. To the yellow zone, which is much further - up to 1.5 hours. And to the red zone - 2 hours.
